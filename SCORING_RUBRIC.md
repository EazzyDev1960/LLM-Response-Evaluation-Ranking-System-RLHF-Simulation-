# Scoring Rubric

## Evaluation Scale

| Score | Rating | Description |
|-------|---------|-------------|
|10|Exceptional|Complete, accurate, well-reasoned and fully satisfies the prompt.|
|9|Excellent|Minor improvements possible but highly reliable.|
|8|Very Good|Strong response with small weaknesses.|
|7|Good|Useful but contains noticeable issues.|
|6|Fair|Partially correct with multiple weaknesses.|
|5|Average|Adequate but lacks depth or accuracy.|
|4|Poor|Major reasoning or factual issues.|
|3|Very Poor|Fails most evaluation criteria.|
|2|Critical|Highly inaccurate or misleading.|
|1|Unacceptable|Unsafe, fabricated or unusable.|

---

## Evaluation Categories

- Instruction Following
- Correctness
- Reasoning Quality
- Writing Quality
- Clarity
- Completeness
- Brevity
- Factual Accuracy
- Hallucination Detection
- Safety
- Bias
- Overall Quality

---

## Final Score

Overall Score = Weighted average of all evaluation dimensions.
