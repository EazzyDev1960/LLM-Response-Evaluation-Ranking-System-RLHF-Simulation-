# Hallucination Detection Framework

## Objective

Identify unsupported, fabricated or unverifiable information generated by language models.

---

## Detection Categories

- Fabricated Facts
- Invented Statistics
- False Citations
- Unsupported Claims
- Incorrect Reasoning
- Misleading Statements
- Outdated Information

---

## Verification Workflow

Claim

↓

Evidence Search

↓

Source Verification

↓

Cross Validation

↓

Confidence Assessment

↓

Final Verdict

---

## Severity Levels

|Level|Meaning|
|-----|-------|
|Low|Minor unsupported detail|
|Medium|Multiple unsupported statements|
|High|Critical fabricated information|

---

## Recommended Action

- Accept
- Minor Revision
- Major Revision
- Reject
